VERIFICATION

To verify the embedded binary:

1. Download the official release from:
   https://github.com/The-Uprooted-Project/uprooted/releases

2. Download the checksums file:
   https://github.com/The-Uprooted-Project/uprooted/releases/download/v<VERSION>/checksums.txt

3. Compare the SHA256 hash of the embedded uprooted.exe against the checksums file:
   Get-FileHash .\tools\uprooted.exe -Algorithm SHA256

4. The hash should match the entry for Uprooted-<VERSION>-windows-amd64.exe in checksums.txt.
